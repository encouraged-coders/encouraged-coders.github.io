# enCouraged Coders

Collaborative Software & Systems Engineering

## Services

I offer coaching, training, and hands-on workshops around **Software and Systems Engineering**—practical, collaborative, and tailored to your context.

**Typical clients**

- Individuals or engineering teams
- Small to mid-sized companies and startups (Germany-based)
- Building mechatronic systems/products with a high software share

**How I can help**

- Introduce, renew, and operationalize methods and toolchains in your software & systems engineering process
- Improve clarity and alignment through lightweight, reviewable documentation and architecture practices
- Strengthen collaboration in distributed teams with transparent, auditable communication patterns
- Establish or improve CI/CD for software, specifications, and documentation
- Apply modern AI workflows (LLMs, agents) responsibly to the above

## Topics & Methods

- Documentation-as-Code
- Architectural design with **arc42** and **aim42** (views on systems and software)
- Working practices and structures aligned with / inspired by **Automotive SPICE 4.0**
- Inner Source (transparent, asynchronous, distributed, review- and audit-ready collaboration)
- Continuous Integration / Deployment / Delivery for software as well as specifications and documentation
- Agile at team and scaling level: **SCRUM**, **Essential SAFe 6.0**
- AI in engineering workflows (LLMs, agents) integrated into the above methods
- Integration into existing IT infrastructures
